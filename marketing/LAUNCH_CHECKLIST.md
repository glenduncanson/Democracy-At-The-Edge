# Launch Checklist — Democracy at the Edge

## Wait for these two emails from KDP

- [ ] **Paperback** is live — typically 24–72hr after publishing (often faster for republishes). Subject usually starts with *"Your book is live on Amazon"*.
- [ ] **Kindle eBook update** is live — same window. ASIN remains B0GX2ZVDV7.

## When the paperback email lands

1. [ ] Open the Amazon listing — confirm both Kindle and Paperback formats appear on the same product page.
2. [ ] Test the buy redirect: visit https://glenduncanson.github.io/Democracy-At-The-Edge/buy and confirm it lands on the Amazon page.
3. [ ] Order author copies:
   - KDP Bookshelf → "..." menu next to the paperback → **Order Author Copies**
   - Marketplace: **amazon.co.uk** (cheapest UK shipping)
   - Quantity: 5–10 to start
   - Cost: ~£6–7 per copy + shipping (no royalty taken)
